Rules

  1. The rocket will be launched when the amount of coins in the room reaches a certain level.
  2. General gifts: 10 coins = 10 progress points. Lucky gifts: 10 coins = 1 progress point.
  3. Rewards vary by Dragon Egg level. The top 10 contributors and the room owner may receive rewards from the corresponding reward pool.
  4. Coins and Game Coins are one-time rewards. Vehicle, Avatar Frame, and Chat Bubble rewards are valid for the listed number of days.
  5. Except that the VIP2 reward for Level 1 is valid for 3 days, all other privileges and VIP rewards are valid for 7 days.
  6. The rocket progress will be reset at 00:00 (GMT+8) every day.
  7. The final interpretation of the activity belongs to the official team. Apple is not involved in this activity in any way.
